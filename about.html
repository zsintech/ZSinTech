About Me

Hi, I'm Zheen!

My journey has taken an unconventional path toward technology, blending activism, science communication, and technology. Coming from a family of passionate journalists and communicators, my initial interests leaned toward medicine, driven by a desire to make tangible impacts on society. However, experiences in combating misinformation during the Covid-19 pandemic highlighted for me the critical need for effective technological tools and platforms.

Recognizing the intersection of activism, science communication, and technology, I transitioned into software engineering. This journey allows me to merge my passion for social advocacy with practical technical skills, actively contributing to science communication initiatives and various community-focused projects. At university, I co-founded the Adventure Club to promote biodiversity awareness and was actively involved in co-founding multiple projects that enhance digital literacy and environmental consciousness. Currently, I work as a remote research assistant employing AI to enhance science communication and am engaged in research utilizing digital literacy strategies to combat human trafficking.

What I Do

Currently, I specialize in tech content creation, IT project management, and developing innovative solutions that empower communities and encourage diversity in STEM fields. My approach combines clear and empathetic communication, structured methodologies, and creative storytelling to drive meaningful change.

Through my collaborative efforts, I leverage AI and data-driven strategies to address global challenges effectively.
