Enhancing Science Communication Through AI Tools: A Comparative Analysis

Journal of Science Communication, 2023

Zheen Stran, et al.

This paper explores the effectiveness of AI-powered tools in improving science communication to non-expert audiences. Using a mixed-methods approach, we evaluated how different AI assistance models impact comprehension, engagement, and information retention across diverse demographic groups.

Digital Literacy as a Tool Against Human Trafficking: Intervention Strategies

International Journal of Digital Ethics, 2022

Zheen Stran, Jane Doe, John Smith

This research investigates how digital literacy programs can be leveraged as preventative measures against human trafficking. The paper presents findings from a two-year study examining the impact of targeted digital education initiatives in vulnerable communities.

Environmental Awareness Through Technology: Biodiversity Monitoring Applications

Environmental Technology & Innovation, 2022

Zheen Stran, Alex Johnson

This paper examines how mobile technology can engage non-specialists in biodiversity monitoring. We present a case study of the Adventure Club initiative, analyzing how technology-mediated experiences in nature foster environmental consciousness and scientific literacy.
